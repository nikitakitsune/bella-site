Jewelry Brand

Bella BRANDING DESIGN

Bella's curated collection marries classic elegance with modern allure, offering a range of handcrafted jewelry that transcends trends and captures the essence of sophistication. From delicately designed necklaces that grace your neckline with grace to statement rings that evoke a sense of glamour, each piece at Bella Jewelry tells a unique story. They celebrate the brilliance of carefully chosen gemstones, infusing designs with a touch of luxury. Bella Jewelry invites you to discover adornments that not only complement your style but also become an expression of your individuality.

Brand Color Palette

The pristine purity of white serves as the canvas, embodying the brand's commitment to elegance. A touch of enchanting orange infuses vibrancy and energy, adding a playful yet refined element to each piece. Beige, reminiscent of timeless simplicity, brings a sense of warmth, complementing the luxurious gemstones and enhancing the overall aesthetic. Black, the epitome of sophistication, weaves a thread of bold contrast throughout, creating a harmonious blend that defines the brand's identity. Brand Typography

The primary font, Elanor, gracefully takes center stage, with its timeless serifs and delicate strokes. Elanor imparts a sense of classic charm, reflecting the enduring beauty found in each piece of jewelry. Complementing this, the secondary font, Brandon Grotesque, adds a modern twist with its clean lines and contemporary aesthetic. Together, these fonts dance in perfect harmony, narrating the story of Bella Jewelry with every stroke and curve.
